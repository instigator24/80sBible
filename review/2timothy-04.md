### 2 Timothy 4:1-4
**WEB:** I command you therefore before God and the Lord Jesus Christ, who will judge the living and the dead at his appearing and his Kingdom: preach the word; be urgent in season and out of season; reprove, rebuke, and exhort, with all patience and teaching. For the time will come when they will not listen to the sound doctrine, but, having itching ears, will heap up for themselves teachers after their own lusts; and will turn away their ears from the truth, and turn away to fables.
**Draft:** So I'm orderin' you, right in front of God and the Lord Jesus Christ, who's gonna judge the livin' and the dead when He shows up with His Kingdom: preach the word; stay on it whether it's convenient or not; call stuff out, rebuke, encourage, with total patience and teachin'. 'Cause the time's comin' when folks won't put up with sound doctrine no more — instead, itchin' for somethin' new, they'll stack up teachers who tell 'em whatever they wanna hear, and turn their ears away from the truth, chasin' after made-up fables instead.
