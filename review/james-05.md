### James 5:17-20

He prayed again, and the sky gave rain, and the earth produced its fruit. Brothers, if any among you wanders from the truth and someone turns him back, let him know that he who turns a sinner from the error of his way will save a soul from death and will cover a multitude of sins.
**Draft:** Elijah was just a regular dude, same as us, and he prayed hard for it not to rain, and it didn't rain a drop for three whole years and six months. Brothers, if anybody among you wanders off from the truth and somebody brings him back, let that guy know: whoever turns a sinner back from his wrong path saves a soul from death and covers up a whole mountain of sins.
