### Revelation 12:1-4
**WEB:** A great sign was seen in heaven: a woman clothed with the sun, and the moon under her feet, and on her head a crown of twelve stars. She was with child. She cried out in pain, laboring to give birth. Another sign was seen in heaven. Behold, a great red dragon, having seven heads and ten horns, and on his heads seven crowns. His tail drew one third of the stars of the sky, and threw them to the earth. The dragon stood before the woman who was about to give birth, so that when she gave birth he might devour her child.
**Draft:** Then this epic sign shows up in heaven: a woman wrapped in the sun like a robe, the moon parked under her feet, and a crown of twelve stars sitting on her head -- straight-up psychedelic. She's pregnant, and she's crying out in pain, deep in labor, about to give birth. Then another sign shows up in heaven, and check this out -- a huge red dragon, seven heads, ten horns, seven crowns stacked on those heads. Way gnarly. His tail sweeps a third of the stars right outta the sky and chucks 'em down to earth. This dragon plants himself right in front of the woman who's about to give birth, just waiting to gobble up her baby the second it's born.

**Draft:** She gives birth to a son, a baby boy who's destined to rule every nation with an iron rod, no messing around. The woman bolts out into the wilderness, to a spot God already had prepped for her, where they'll take care of her for one thousand two hundred sixty days. Meanwhile, war breaks out up in the sky -- Michael and his angels squaring off against the dragon, and the dragon and his angels fighting right back. But the dragon's crew didn't stand a chance -- they got stomped, and there wasn't any room left for 'em in heaven anymore. Outta there for good.

### Revelation 12:9-12

He was thrown down to the earth, and his angels were thrown down with him. I heard a loud voice in heaven, saying, “Now the salvation, the power, and the Kingdom of our God, and the authority of his Christ has come; for the accuser of our brothers has been thrown down, who accuses them before our God day and night. They overcame him because of the Lamb’s blood, and because of the word of their testimony. They didn’t love their life, even to death. Therefore rejoice, heavens, and you who dwell in them. Then I hear this booming voice in heaven going, "Now it's here -- the salvation, the power, and the Kingdom of our God, and the authority of his Christ! 'Cause the one who's been ratting out our brothers, accusing 'em in front of God day and night, just got thrown down for good. They beat him 'cause of the Lamb's blood and 'cause of the word they testified to -- they didn't cling to their own lives, not even when it meant dying for it. So party on, heavens, and everybody living up there! But way harsh news for earth and sea, 'cause the devil's come down to you, boiling with rage, knowing full well his time's almost up."

The serpent spewed water out of his mouth after the woman like a river, that he might cause her to be carried away by the stream. The earth helped the woman, and the earth opened its mouth and swallowed up the river which the dragon spewed out of his mouth.
**Draft:** Once the dragon realizes he's been dumped down to earth, he goes straight after the woman who had the baby boy, hunting her down hard. The serpent, being bogus as ever, spews a whole river of water outta his mouth after her, trying to sweep her away in the flood. But the earth steps up and helps the woman out -- cracks open its mouth and swallows that whole river right down, gone.
