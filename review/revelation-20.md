### Revelation 20:1-4
**WEB:** I saw an angel coming down out of heaven, having the key of the abyss and a great chain in his hand. He seized the dragon, the old serpent, which is the devil and Satan, who deceives the whole inhabited earth, and bound him for a thousand years, and cast him into the abyss, and shut it, and sealed it over him, that he should deceive the nations no more, until the thousand years were finished. After this, he must be freed for a short time. I saw thrones, and they sat on them, and judgment was given to them. I saw the souls of those who had been beheaded for the testimony of Jesus, and for the word of God, and such as didn’t worship the beast nor his image, and didn’t receive the mark on their forehead and on their hand. They lived and reigned with Christ for a thousand years.
**Draft:** I saw an angel coming down outta heaven, holding the key to the bottomless pit and a massive chain in his hand. He grabbed the dragon -- that old serpent, the one they call the devil and Satan, the dude who's been conning the whole world since forever -- and chained him up for a thousand years. He threw him down into the pit, slammed it shut, and sealed it tight over him, so he couldn't fool the nations anymore till the thousand years were up. After that, he's gotta get loose for a little while, no way around it. Then I saw thrones, with people sitting on 'em, and they got handed the power to judge. I saw the souls of everybody who'd been beheaded for standing up for Jesus and for God's word -- the ones who never bowed down to the beast or his statue, never took his mark on their hand or forehead. They came back to life and reigned with Christ for a thousand years, fer sure.

### Revelation 20:5-8
**WEB:** The rest of the dead didn’t live until the thousand years were finished. This is the first resurrection. Blessed and holy is he who has part in the first resurrection. Over these, the second death has no power, but they will be priests of God and of Christ, and will reign with him one thousand years. And after the thousand years, Satan will be released from his prison, and he will come out to deceive the nations which are in the four corners of the earth, Gog and Magog, to gather them together to the war; the number of whom is as the sand of the sea.
**Draft:** The rest of the dead didn't come back to life till those thousand years wrapped up -- this right here is the first resurrection. Blessed and righteous is anybody who gets in on that first resurrection, 'cause the second death's got zero power over them. They're gonna be priests serving God and Christ, reigning with him the whole thousand years. But once the thousand years are done, Satan gets sprung loose from lockup, and he comes out swinging, ready to con the nations again -- Gog and Magog, from every corner of the earth -- rounding 'em all up for one last war, a crowd so massive it's like counting grains of sand on the beach.

### Revelation 20:9-12
**WEB:** They went up over the width of the earth, and surrounded the camp of the saints, and the beloved city. Fire came down out of heaven from God and devoured them. The devil who deceived them was thrown into the lake of fire and sulfur, where the beast and the false prophet are also. They will be tormented day and night forever and ever. I saw a great white throne, and him who sat on it, from whose face the earth and the heaven fled away. There was found no place for them. I saw the dead, the great and the small, standing before the throne, and they opened books. Another book was opened, which is the book of life. The dead were judged out of the things which were written in the books, according to their works.
**Draft:** They spread out across the whole earth and surrounded the camp of God's people, the city he loves. But fire rained down straight outta heaven from God and wiped 'em out, no contest. The devil who'd been conning everybody got tossed into the lake of fire and sulfur, right alongside the beast and the false prophet, and they're gonna get tormented day and night, forever and ever -- way harsh, no bogus way out of that one. Then I saw a great white throne, and the one sitting on it, and his presence was so heavy that earth and sky just took off running, and there wasn't a place left for 'em to hide. I saw the dead, big shots and nobodies alike, standing in front of that throne, and books got opened up. Another book got opened too -- the book of life. And the dead got judged by what was written in those books, based on what they'd actually done.

### Revelation 20:13-15
**WEB:** The sea gave up the dead who were in it. Death and Hades gave up the dead who were in them. They were judged, each one according to his works. Death and Hades were thrown into the lake of fire. This is the second death, the lake of fire. If anyone was not found written in the book of life, he was cast into the lake of fire.
**Draft:** The sea gave up every dead person stuck inside it. Death and Hades gave up their dead too, and everybody got judged according to what they'd done, no exceptions. Then Death and Hades themselves got thrown into the lake of fire -- that's the second death, that lake of fire, and there's nothing worse waiting past that. And if anybody's name wasn't found written down in the book of life, that was it, no appeal -- straight into the lake of fire.
