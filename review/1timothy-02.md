### 1 Timothy 2:1-4
**WEB:** I exhort therefore, first of all, that petitions, prayers, intercessions, and givings of thanks, be made for all men: for kings and all who are in high places; that we may lead a tranquil and quiet life in all godliness and reverence. For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Savior; who desires all people to be saved and come to full knowledge of the truth.
**Draft:** So first off, I'm pushin' you to make sure requests, prayers, pleadin' for others, and givin' thanks all get made for everybody — for kings and everybody in high positions — so we can live a peaceful, quiet life, all godly and reverent. That's good, and it's exactly what pleases God our Savior, who wants every single person saved and full-up on the truth.

### 1 Timothy 2:5-8
**WEB:** For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus, who gave himself as a ransom for all; the testimony in its own times; to which I was appointed a preacher and an apostle (I am telling the truth in Christ, not lying), a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and truth. I desire therefore that the men in every place pray, lifting up holy hands without anger and doubting.
**Draft:** 'Cause there's one God, and one middleman between God and people — the man Christ Jesus, who handed Himself over as a ransom for everybody; a testimony given right on time. That's what I got appointed a preacher and apostle for (I'm tellin' you the truth in Christ, no lie), a teacher to the Gentiles in faith and truth. So I want the guys everywhere to pray, liftin' up holy hands, no anger, no arguin'.

### 1 Timothy 2:9-12
**WEB:** In the same way, that women also adorn themselves in decent clothing, with modesty and propriety; not just with braided hair, gold, pearls, or expensive clothing; but (which becomes women professing godliness) with good works. Let a woman learn in quietness with full submission. But I don’t permit a woman to teach, nor to exercise authority over a man, but to be in quietness.
**Draft:** Same deal, women should dress modest and proper, not just chasin' fancy braids, gold, pearls, or expensive threads — but instead (fittin' for women who claim to be godly) dressed up in good deeds. Let a woman learn quietly, all the way submissive. I'm not lettin' a woman teach or boss around a man — she oughta stay quiet.

### 1 Timothy 2:13-15
**WEB:** For Adam was first formed, then Eve. Adam wasn’t deceived, but the woman, being deceived, has fallen into disobedience; but she will be saved through her childbearing, if they continue in faith, love, and sanctification with sobriety.
**Draft:** 'Cause Adam got formed first, then Eve. Adam didn't get conned, but the woman got conned and fell into disobedience. But she'll get saved through havin' kids, long as they keep stickin' with faith, love, and holiness, stayin' level-headed about it.
